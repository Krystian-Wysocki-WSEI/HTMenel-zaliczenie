What is White Knuckle?
If you don't know, then why are you even here?
White Knuckle is an indie horror game created by Dark Machine Games, composed of Holly Jencka (lead programmer and developer), Quinn Steagall (artist, animator), and Jack Folkner (sound designer, musician); and published by DreadXP. It has been released in the Early Access model in April 2025.
The main gameplay of White Knuckle involves trying to escape an enormous underground megastructure. The player has to climb up through multiple zones inside of the Substructure, later escaping into the bigger encompassing Superstructure, while avoiding the structure's multiple alien, more-or-less hostile denizens. The game also involves elements of exploration, inventory management, and limited meta progression. White Knuckle is currently in early access, and will remain so while new content is being added. It can currently be purchased on Steam for 14.99$.
