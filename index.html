What is White Knuckle?
If you don't know, then why are you even here?
White Knuckle is an indie horror game created by Dark Machine Games, composed of Holly Jencka (lead programmer and developer), Quinn Steagall (artist, animator), and Jack Folkner (sound designer, musician); and published by DreadXP.
